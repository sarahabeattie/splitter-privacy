Splitter Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Overview

Splitter ("we", "our", or "the app") is a receipt-splitting app that helps you divide expenses with friends. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Access

Camera

Splitter accesses your device camera solely to photograph receipts for item recognition. Photos are processed entirely on your device using on-device text recognition. Receipt images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

Photo Library

With your permission, Splitter can import receipt photos from your photo library for item recognition. Imported photos are processed on-device and are not uploaded.

Contacts

With your permission, Splitter can read your device contacts to help you quickly add people to a split. We access first names, last names, and phone numbers. Contact data is used only within the app to pre-fill names and phone numbers for SMS messaging. Contact data is never uploaded, shared, or stored outside your device.

Data Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This includes:

  • Your first name and payment preferences
  • Split history (items, people, amounts)
  • Recently used names for quick access
  • Receipt photos you have captured

Receipt data, contacts, and split history remain entirely on your device. Limited subscription data is shared with RevenueCat as described below.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

Splitter uses RevenueCat to manage the optional Splitter Pro subscription. RevenueCat receives an anonymous subscriber identifier, purchase transaction details (such as product purchased and purchase date), and basic device information (device model, operating system version, and vendor identifier) to verify and manage your subscription status. RevenueCat does not receive your name, contacts, receipt data, or payment method details. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Payment Apps

Splitter may link to third-party payment apps (Venmo, Cash App, Zelle) at your request to facilitate payments. These links open the respective apps on your device. We do not share any data with these services. Your use of third-party payment apps is governed by their respective privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

Splitter offers an optional annual subscription ("Splitter Pro") processed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple and is not accessible to us. RevenueCat receives purchase transaction details to manage your subscription status, but does not receive your billing or payment method information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles payment data.

Analytics and Tracking

Splitter does not use analytics, advertising, or behavioral tracking frameworks. We do not collect usage data or behavioral information. We do not use cookies or similar technologies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Limited data is shared with RevenueCat solely for subscription management as described above. No other data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Splitter is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

All local data can be deleted at any time by deleting the Splitter app from your device. You can also clear individual split history from within the app. To request deletion of subscription data held by RevenueCat, contact us at the email below.

Your Rights

Since nearly all data remains on your device, you have full control over your information at all times. For any data held by third-party services (RevenueCat), you may contact us to request access or deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
