Splitter Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Overview

Splitter ("we", "our", or "the app") is a receipt-splitting app that helps you divide expenses with friends. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we access, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Access

Camera

Splitter accesses your device camera solely to photograph receipts for item recognition. Photos are processed entirely on your device using on-device text recognition. Receipt images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

Contacts

With your permission, Splitter can read your device contacts to help you quickly add people to a split. We access first names, last names, and phone numbers. Contact data is used only within the app to pre-fill names and phone numbers for SMS messaging. Contact data is never uploaded, shared, or stored outside your device.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This includes:

  • Your first name and payment preferences
  • Split history (items, people, amounts)
  • Recently used names for quick access
  • Receipt photos you have captured

No data is transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties. We do not operate any backend servers or databases.

Third-Party Services

Splitter may link to third-party payment apps (Venmo, Cash App, Zelle) at your request to facilitate payments. These links open the respective apps on your device. We do not share any data with these services. Your use of third-party payment apps is governed by their respective privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

Splitter offers an optional annual subscription ("Splitter Pro") processed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple and is not accessible to us. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles payment data.

Analytics and Tracking

Splitter does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral information. We do not use cookies or similar technologies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Period.

Children's Privacy

Splitter is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the Splitter app from your device. You can also clear individual split history from within the app.

Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device and we have no access to it, you have full control over your information at all times. No request to us is necessary to access, modify, or delete your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at ncbeattiesupport@proton.me.
